About Us

Welcome to Motoworld, your number one source for helmets in the Philippines. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of helmets, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2004, Motoworld has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for providing the best safety gear for motorcycle enthusiasts drove us to do tons of research, so that Motoworld can offer you the world's most advanced helmets. We now serve customers all over the Philippines and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
The Motoworld Team
